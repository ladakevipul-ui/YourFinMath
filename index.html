Frequently asked questions

Everything here is designed to reduce hesitation and help visitors feel confident using the calculators for important financial decisions.

Are these calculators free to use?

Yes. YourFinMath gives users instant access to 35+ finance calculators without signup, so they can plan important decisions without friction or hidden paywalls.

Can I trust the results for day-to-day planning?

The calculators are built around consistent formula-based logic to help users estimate EMIs, returns, taxes and take-home salary quickly. They are ideal for planning, comparison and shortlisting next steps.

Is my financial information private?

Yes. This version works directly in the browser and does not require an account, which helps create a simpler and more privacy-friendly experience for users exploring personal financial numbers.

Is this useful only for investors?

No. YourFinMath is useful for home buyers, salaried employees, freelancers, families building savings, and anyone comparing money decisions across loans, taxes, investments and future planning.

Are the tax and planning tools relevant for India?

Yes. The experience is written for Indian users and includes calculators for income tax (FY 2026-27), GST, salary, PPF, EPF, NPS, EMI and other common financial decisions in India.

Why does the site feel product-focused instead of blog-like?

Because trust grows when the experience is clean, fast and predictable. The design is intentionally structured like a premium fintech product so users can focus on decisions, not distractions.
