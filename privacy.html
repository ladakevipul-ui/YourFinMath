Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly how YourFinMath handles your information — no legal jargon, just plain language.

📅 Last updated: April 2026
✅ No data sent to servers ✅ No personal tracking ✅ Browser-only calculations ✅ No account required
Section 01

Information We Collect

YourFinMath is a browser-based tool. All calculator inputs you enter (loan amounts, salary, investment figures, etc.) are processed entirely within your browser and are never sent to our servers.

We may automatically collect limited technical data through standard server logs or analytics tools, including:

  • Your browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic region (country/state level)
Section 02

Cookies

We may use cookies or local storage to remember your preferences (such as dark mode). We do not use cookies to track you personally across the web.

If we integrate Google AdSense or Google Analytics in the future, those services may set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Section 03

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — for understanding site traffic
  • Google AdSense — for displaying relevant ads (when enabled)
  • Affiliate links — links to Bankbazaar, Zerodha, Groww, Angel One etc. (we may earn a commission if you sign up through these links)
Section 04

How We Use Information

Any data we collect is used only to:

  • Improve the website's performance and content
  • Understand which calculators are most useful
  • Display relevant advertising (when AdSense is active)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Section 05

Children's Privacy

YourFinMath is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Section 06

Data Security

Since your financial inputs never leave your browser, there is no risk of your financial data being stored or leaked by us. We take reasonable measures to keep the website secure.

Section 07

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (use browser extensions like uBlock Origin)
Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Section 09

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: hello@yourfinmath.com
